Coffee

Antioquia

Balanceado - Balanced

Delight in the harmonious combination of notes of velvety brown sugar, succulent plum, and crisp apple notes, elevating your coffee experience to new heights. Indulge in this exquisite brew that captures the essence of Antioquia's breathtaking landscapes in every sip.

Nariño

Suave - Mild

Exquisite mild coffee, cultivated in the breathtaking mountains of Nariño. Savor the invigorating blend of zesty lemon and indulgent brown sugar, as each sip transports you to the lush coffee plantations nestled amidst some of Colombia's majestic mountains.

Sierra Nevada

Fuerte - Strong

Discover the bold essence of Sierra Nevada's coffee. Immerse yourself in the robust symphony of flavors, where notes of rich dark chocolate, sweet caramel, and roasted walnuts intertwine. Embrace the captivating taste of this exceptional brew, reminiscent of the enchanting Sierra Nevada landscape.
